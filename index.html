Check Your Air Quality

Hello! My name is Carl-Henry Kamp and I am a student at La Scuola International in San Francisco, California
and am graduating in 2024.

This site is part of something called the Middle Years Program project, or MYP project for short, in which design a project in order to help a community.
I chose to create a website, in order to make information about air quality more accessible
to people who require it. Such as people with conditions such as asthma or COPD.

Here's a little backstory:
In December of 2023, I moved to a small village in the northeast of Italy called Tarvisio.
I moved to Tarvisio in order to continue ski racing at a very high level, which was no
longer possible in the United States.

Image of Tarvisio ^^^
Although everything was nice, there was one major issue: the air quality.
Every single time you took a breath, you could taste the air pollution.
This made me curious; how is the air pollution so bad? What are the causes? And how does this affect me and the village?

This curiosity is what led me to choosing air quality and it's connection with your health for my MYP project.
This site is my action for the MYP and my goal is to make information regarding air quality and its affects on your health
more clear and simple to understand.
